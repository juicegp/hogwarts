Our Mission

At Hogwarts, it is our mission to train young witches and wizards in magic. Our courses are designed to give our students an understanding of their world and the magic held within. They will learn transfiguration, history of magic, potions, and many other necessary magical skill that will help them succeed when they leave Hogwarts. This school intends to train every young person who enters the castle to be the best witch or wizard they can be.

Hogwarts, found in Scotland, was founded in the 10th century by Helena Hufflepuff, Godric Gryffindor, Rowena Ravenclaw, and Salazar Slytherin. The school that they built is a pace for all young witches and wizards to come to and learn about their abilities. We strive to give our students the best education that bring them wonderful opportunities. Our castle is open to all magical people, and we hope that they will find a home here. This castle is a place of education, wonder, and exploration, and we can't wait to grow with our students every year.
