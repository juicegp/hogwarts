Our Mission

At Hogwarts, it is our mission to train young witches and wizards in magic. Our courses are designed to give our students an understanding of their world and the magic held within. They will learn transfiguration, history of magic, potions, and many other necessary magical skill that will help them succeed when they leave Hogwarts. This school intends to train every young person who enters the castle to be the best witch or wizard they can be.

The Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, located in Scotland, was founded in the 10th century A.D. by Godric Gryffindor, Helga Hufflpuff, Rowena Ravenclaw, and Salazar Slytherin. These great witches and wizards created a place of educaiton, exploration, and wonder, where all magical humans can find a home. Through the hard work of headmasters, professors, and care takers, Hogwarts was, and remains, a magical place where students can learn!
