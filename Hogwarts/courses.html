Our Courses

Hogwarts offers many courses to train our students in the ways of magic. Every student must take the same courses in first and second year. Starting with third year, students have some choice over which courses they take, and they get more choice throughout their years so that they can study in an area of their interest. The courses that are provided here are of the highest quality and will effecitvely train students for their time after Hogwarts.
